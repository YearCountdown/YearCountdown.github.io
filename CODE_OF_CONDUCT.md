# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone.

## Standards

- Be respectful and constructive
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and addressed appropriately.

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) v2.1.
